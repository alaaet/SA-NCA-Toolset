Purpose

This standard aims to define the detailed cybersecurity requirements
related to the physical security of <organization name>’s premises, buildings
and assets to minimize cybersecurity risks resulting from internal and external
threats at <organization name> in order to preserve confidentiality, integrity and
availability.

The requirements in this standard are aligned with the cybersecurity
requirements issued by the National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA) in addition
to other related cybersecurity legal and regulatory requirements.

Scope

These standard covers <organization name>’s premises, buildings and
assets in <organization to add in physical locations as appropriate, such as data
centers, offices and warehouses> and applies to all <organization name>
personnel (employees and contractors).
