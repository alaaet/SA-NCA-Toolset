# Cybersecurity Guidelines for Internet of Things (IoT)

The National Cybersecurity Authority, as the national entity responsible for cybersecurity in the Kingdom, establishes policies, governance mechanisms, frameworks, standards, controls, and guidelines related to cybersecurity, circulating them to the relevant stakeholders and ensuring subsequent compliance.

Issuing the “Cybersecurity Guidelines for the Internet of Things” (IoT) embeds IoT cybersecurity best practices that are recommended to be applied in all ogrnaiztions that utilize IoT technologies across the kingdom IoT, as well as cybersecurity principles in manufacturers' products and services, thereby reducing cybersecurity risks.
