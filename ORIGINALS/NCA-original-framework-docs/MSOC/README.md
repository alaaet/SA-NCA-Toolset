# National Policy for Managed Security Operations Centers

The National Cybersecurity Authority, as the national authority responsible for cybersecurity in the Kingdom, establishes policies, governance mechanisms, frameworks, standards, controls, and guidelines related to cybersecurity; as well as circulating them with relevant stakeholders, following up their compliance, and updating them. The National Policy for Managed Security Operations Centers (MSOC) aims to provide national organizations with access to reliable, mature and high-quality MSOC services.
