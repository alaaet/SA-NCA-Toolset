## üîç Key Roles (The Usual Suspects)
### 1. The CISO (`LWD-L-001`)
*   **Job**: The Boss. Sets strategy, takes the heat when things go wrong.
*   **Needs**: Leadership, Business Acumen, and deep technical understanding.

### 2. The SOC Analyst (`PD-D-001`)
*   **Job**: The Watcher. Stares at screens, detects threats.
*   **Needs**: Log analysis, SIEM knowledge, patience.

### 3. The Pentester (`PD-VA-002`)
*   **Job**: The Hacker. Breaks things (legally) to find holes.
*   **Needs**: Scripting, OS internals, creative thinking.

### 4. The GRC Specialist (`GRCL-GRC-002`)
*   **Job**: The Auditor. Ensures compliance (checks the boxes).
*   **Needs**: Knowledge of ECC, ISO 27001, and persistence.
